                <h4><strong>Weekly Reading Programme</strong></h4>
            </summary>
            <p>This is a Friday weekly reading programme using newspapers, reading articles and subscribed books. The purpose is to provide ample opportunity for students to read MTL reading materials with follow up activities in language learning and current affairs discussions.</p>

        <details name="keyprog">
            <summary id="imo">
                <h4><strong>In My Opinion (IMO)</strong></h4>
            </summary>
            <p>As an extension from the reading programme, Upper Secondary students are to provide opinions on current affairs on a weekly or a fortnightly basis. This is to encourage students to improve their competency in expressing opinions as part of oral conversation.</p>
